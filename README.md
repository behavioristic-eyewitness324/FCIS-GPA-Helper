## 🎯 About the software

FCIS-GPA-Helper gives you the results you need for your academic performance. This tool monitors your current credits and grades to determine your GPA and CGPA. You do not need to perform manual calculations or use complex spreadsheets. The application automates the process to ensure you see your progress at a glance.

This software works for students at the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence at Ain Shams University. It reads your grade information from your student portal and updates your cumulative records. You save time and remove the risk of human error in your calculations.

## ⚙️ Using the application

Once you open the software, follow these steps to see your grades:

1.  **Login**: Enter your official university credentials into the provided fields. The connection is secure and uses the same encryption as the official web portal.
2.  **Sync Data**: Click the Sync button. The software connects to the server and retrieves your current transcript details. This process takes a few seconds.
3.  **View Results**: The dashboard displays your GPA for the current semester and your total CGPA calculated across all terms.
4.  **Export data**: If you need a copy of your results for your records, click the Export button to save the information as a text file.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app store my password?**
No. The password stays on your device. We do not transmit or save your login details in any form.

**Why does the app show a warning during install?**
Windows sometimes displays an alert for new software. This is a standard procedure for applications not downloaded from the Microsoft Store. You can safely select More Info and then Run Anyway.

**What happens if I forget my password?**
This application uses your existing portal credentials. If you lose your password, you must reset it through the official university website first. Login to the portal to confirm your new password works before you attempt to sync in the application.

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version becomes available, the application notifies you during startup. Follow the prompt to download the latest setup file. Running the new installer replaces the older version automatically.

**Does this work if I have a pending grade?**
Yes. The software calculates your performance based on existing data. If a grade is missing, it displays the GPA based on the information currently available at the university portal.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If the application fails to fetch data, verify your internet connection. Check if you can access the student portal via your web browser. If the portal is down for maintenance, the application will not be able to retrieve your grades until the portal is back online. Ensure you do not have a firewall blocking the application from accessing the network. If you continue to see errors, restart the application and check your login details for typos.
